My Journey Journal

This website is intended to be used to document my journey with chronic illness as a young adult, and share resources to help other young adults struggling with the same.

What is a Journey Journal?

A Journey Journal is a journal, either online or paper where a person documents their journey through a time in their life. The most common journey journals you can find on the internet discuss the topics of:

  • Grief/Loss of a loved one
  • The life of a pet
  • Documenting life with an illness/recovery
  • Getting into a new hobby
  • Moving

Why am I making a Journey Journal?

I am making a journey journal to not only share my story but to provide resources for other disabled folks, or people who wish to learn more about chronic illnesses. Disabilities and conditions are a lot to learn for anyone regardless of your ability to understand, a personal account can help make understanding the impact on young lives easier.

What are my conditions?

I have been diagnosed with POTS and hEDS within the last couple years.
POTS is described as:

"...a form of dysautonomia — a disorder of the autonomic nervous system. This branch of the nervous system regulates functions we don’t consciously control, such as heart rate, blood pressure, sweating and body temperature." - Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome Johns Hopkins

hEDS is described as:
"...is a heritable connective tissue disorder that causes generalized joint hypermobility, joint instability, and chronic pain. hEDS is also associated with a variety of other symptoms and related conditions that affect many different areas of the body." - Hypermobile Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome The Ehlers-Danlos Society
